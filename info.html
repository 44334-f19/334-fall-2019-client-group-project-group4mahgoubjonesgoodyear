Technically, Parti Yorkies are still purebred Yorkshire Terriers, but there is a difference in their coloring. Normal Yorkshire Terriers tend to have blue and/or tan hair, whereas Parti Yorkies will generally be black, white, and tan in color. A Parti Yorkie is any Yorkie with significant white patches or white colors, combined with black and gold is considered a Parti Yorkie.
